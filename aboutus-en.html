History

Our motivation.

As college students, we know how confusing it can be to choose a college degree when you're in school. In the same way that at that time we would have liked to have someone to talk to, now we want to help high school students find their calling. Therefore, we are interested in being able to provide advice and support to future university students during their decision-making process.

Our
 Mission

All students, regardless of their socioeconomic and cultural background, are well-informed to be able to make the right choices for themselves and take ownership of their future.

Our Vision

We seek to contribute to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals proposed by the United Nations.

Goal 4

Quality
Education


Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education regardless of their gender, cultures, and socioeconomic background, while promoting lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Goal 5

Gender
Equality


Achieve gender equality while empowering all women and girls to pursue their desired career. Provide acess to stories of role models and inspiring female students.

Goal 10

Reduce
Inequalities


Ensure all high school students have access to university-life and academical information that allows them to make the correct decision on their undergraduate degree.

Values



Inclusiveness
We believe that different perspectives are the key to building solid foundations. The variety of social backgrounds, points of view, and life experiences that our users provide to the platform are our most valuable asset.
Ownership
We highly encourage our users to take the initiative to bring positive changes into their future. It is important not to wait for others to act. We promote a culture of organizational responsibility while owning and recognizing the impact of our actions.
Generosity
We believe in the fact that generosity is not mainly about what is given, but the spirit in which is given. Therefore, we urge our users to share the most valuable thing a person can have: their own life experiences, knowledge, and time.
